PARAFLOW

Privacy Policy

Effective July 10, 2026

Paraflow is designed for private, offline speech-to-text. This policy explains how the Android app handles information.

1. Offline processing

Paraflow downloads its public speech-recognition model from Hugging Face during initial setup. After installation, recognition runs entirely on your device. Audio and transcribed text are never sent to Paraflow, Hugging Face, a cloud service, or another third party. Network access is used for model delivery only.

2. Permissions and system access

  • Microphone: captures audio only while you choose to dictate. Temporary audio is discarded after transcription.
  • Accessibility Service: detects the focused editable field, displays the dictation control, and inserts only the transcription you request. Nearby text may be inspected to add spacing correctly.
  • Battery optimization exemption: keeps user-enabled cross-app dictation available reliably while Paraflow is in the background.
  • Internet and network state: downloads public model files from huggingface.co and reports progress. No voice, transcription, or history is included in these requests.

3. Local history

Transcription history is stored in the app's private on-device storage. You can copy, delete, or clear it from the Usage tab. Uninstalling the app removes its local data.

4. No tracking or sharing

Optional translation uses Google ML Kit language packs and runs on-device after the selected pack is downloaded. Translation text is not sent to a cloud translation service. Paraflow has no accounts, advertising, analytics, telemetry, or cloud sync.

5. Children's privacy

The app is not directed specifically to children. Paraflow does not transmit personal information off-device or operate user accounts.

6. Changes

Policy changes may be published on this page and included with future app updates.
